How to Play

Bloxd.io is easy to learn but requires practice to master. Here are the basic controls:

Keyboard Controls

  • W / ↑ - Move Forward
  • A / ← - Move Left
  • S / ↓ - Move Backward
  • D / → - Move Right
  • Space - Jump
  • Shift - Sprint

Game Tips

  • Hold space to jump higher
  • Chain jumps to increase speed
  • Look for shortcuts to overtake opponents
  • Master timing and route planning for victory
